WEDDING PLANNING

in Provence & Riverdale-South Of France

Wedding planner, we will assist you in the organisation of your wedding, we will bring a touch of French flair, chic and magic.


A master organiser, WE will take care of the whole wedding planning process from start to finish, to help you with your selection of some vendors and coordinate the Wedding Day so it runs like a dream.


The essential is that the bride and groom do not feel that organising their wedding is a stressful experience. She can sort out everything, the venue, the caterer, the band, the English-speaking officiant, the florist, the wedding stationary, the car, the photographer and even the rehearsal dinner and the post-wedding brunch.Organising a wedding requires attention to detail, the ability to listen, understand and reassure the couple and a certain talent for logistics. We will take charge so you only have to concentrate on the pretty things, choosing the dress, the colour of the flowers...


We will try our best to make the first day of the rest of your lives together a truly memorable event.

WEDDING STAGING

Wedding Staging is now a major part of a wedding so each photo or video shot is optimised with a beautiful bespoke decor. The theme of the wedding will be announced by the stationary and the rest will match harmoniously, the floral decor, the furniture, the table plan, the table settings, photo shoots, the music and lighting, the style of dresses, nothing will be neglected.
